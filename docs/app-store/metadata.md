## Description (max 4000 chars)
```
Indirimbo Zikundwa is a beautiful, fully offline hymnal reader. Carry thousands of beloved hymns and cantiques with you — at church, at home, or anywhere, with no internet needed.

EVERYTHING OFFLINE
• 17 collections and more than 5,000 hymns, available instantly without a connection.
• Open any song the moment you need it — no loading, no ads, no account.

FIND ANY SONG FAST
• Forgiving search that understands typos and accents — type "urukndo" and still find "Urukundo".
• Search by number, title, lyrics or author across every collection.
• Jump straight to a song number within a book while you read.

A READER MADE FOR SINGING
• Clean, printed-hymnal layout with verses and refrains clearly marked.
• Swipe left or right to move between songs.
• Adjust text size and font for the whole app, and pinch to zoom the lyrics.
• Light, sepia and dark themes; keep-screen-on while you sing.
• Select and share any song's text to friends, social media or messaging.

SING TOGETHER (LIVE SHARE)
• Lead a session and everyone on the same Wi-Fi follows the same song and scroll position in real time — no sign-up, no server.
• Others can even follow along in a web browser via a shared link.

YOURS, PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• Mark favorites for quick access.
• Choose your language for the app (English / Français).
• No account, no tracking, no ads. Your data stays on your device.

Indirimbo Zikundwa is made for congregations, choirs and anyone who loves to sing — simple, fast, and always available.
```
